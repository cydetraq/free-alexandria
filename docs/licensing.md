# Licensing and provenance

Each local artifact requires a sibling `provenance.json` or catalog file record containing:

- title, author, translator, edition and publication year;
- source URL and download date;
- rights status and explanation;
- license name, version, URL, and required attribution where relevant;
- whether modifications, conversion, and commercial distribution are permitted;
- SHA-256 hash; and
- reviewer and review date.

Creative Commons `ND` works should normally be preserved unchanged rather than converted to a new format. `NC` works may not be appropriate for devices sold or bundled commercially. Retain a local copy of the governing license or permission statement.
